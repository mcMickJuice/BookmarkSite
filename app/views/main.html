Bookmarky Ya'll

Just a fresh fly site for keeping track of bookmarks and the like

So why create this site? I find myself bookmarking a ton of pages for later reading whether they be articles about a new technology, a tutorial or a cool video on a new framework. However, I find myself trying to organize bookmarks using folders in Chrome and sometimes I'm just too lazy to put a link into the correct folder. In addition, classifying, rating and management becomes a headache. Building this site will help me keep bookmarks in order as well as let me tag and rate each one.
